---

Members share the workspace's projects and settings. Manage them from the
**Members** page.

## Before you begin

You need permission to manage members in the workspace.

## Invite a member

<Steps>
  <Step>

    **Open member settings**

    Open the workspace. Select **Settings → Members**.
  </Step>
  <Step>

    **Send the invitation**

    Select **Invite member**. Enter the person's email address and select
    **Send invitation**.
  </Step>
  <Step>

    **Track the invitation**

    The invitation appears under **Pending invitations**. Shipfox emails a link
    to the person. They can sign in or create an account before using it.
  </Step>
  <Step>

    **Confirm membership**

    After they accept, the person moves to the members list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Revoke an invitation

Open a pending invitation and select **Revoke invitation**. Its email link no
longer grants access. Confirm that the invitation no longer appears under
**Pending invitations**.

## Remove a member

Open a member and select **Remove member**. Check the person and workspace shown
in the dialog, then confirm the removal. The person loses access to the
workspace and disappears from the members list. A member cannot remove their own
account from this page.
